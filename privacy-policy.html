Privacy Policy — SpookyTunes

Last updated: May 8, 2025

SpookyTunes ("we", "our", or "us") is a Pomodoro productivity timer application developed by BigByte. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data. By using SpookyTunes you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to provide the service:

  • Account information: When you register, we collect your email address and display name (full name). If you sign in with Google, we receive your name and email from Google.
  • Usage data: We store your focus session history, streaks, and settings locally on your device and, when you are signed in, in our secure cloud database so your data syncs across devices.
  • Custom sound mixes: Mixes you create are stored in our database and associated with your account.
  • Purchase information: When you buy a premium sound pack, the transaction is processed through Google Play. We receive a confirmation of the purchase (not your payment card details) via RevenueCat to unlock premium content in the app.
  • Device information: We may collect basic device identifiers (e.g. platform type, OS version) for debugging and analytics. We do not collect precise location data.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account and authenticate you securely.
  • To sync your session history, streaks, and custom mixes across devices.
  • To process and verify in-app purchases and unlock premium content.
  • To send transactional emails (e.g. email verification, password reset). We do not send marketing emails without your explicit consent.
  • To improve the app by understanding how features are used (aggregated, non-personal).

3. Data Storage and Security

Your account data is stored securely using Supabase, a cloud database provider that uses industry-standard encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest. Authentication sessions are stored locally on your device using encrypted storage. Purchase data is managed by RevenueCat, which is PCI-compliant. We do not store payment card information at any point.

4. Third-Party Services

SpookyTunes uses the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policy:

  • Supabase (database & authentication) — supabase.com/privacy
  • RevenueCat (in-app purchase management) — revenuecat.com/privacy
  • Google Sign-In — policies.google.com/privacy
  • Google Play Billing — policies.google.com/privacy

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We share data only with the service providers listed above, and only to the extent necessary to operate the app. We may disclose information if required by law or to protect the rights and safety of our users.

6. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law. Local app data (sessions, settings) stored on your device is removed when you uninstall the app.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate data via your account settings.
  • Delete your account and associated data by contacting us at the email below.
  • Export your data upon request.
  • Opt out of any non-essential communications.

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA) or United Kingdom, you also have rights under the GDPR / UK GDPR, including the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

8. Children's Privacy

SpookyTunes is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with their information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
